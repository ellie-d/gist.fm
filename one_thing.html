Here's what good managers actually do: Lessons from 80,000 data points

The Gist

In his research of over 80,000 managers around the world, Marcus Buckingham has discovered that the best ones frequently break the "rules" of management. In this audio he shares how to hire the right people, how to avoid a mistake 50% of managers do when setting expectations, how to use praise to drive performance and why getting personal with your employees matters.

Based on: The One Thing You Need to Know: ... About Great Managing, Great Leading, and Sustained Individual Success

The Author

Marcus Buckingham is a British bestselling author, speaker and researcher who studies the drivers of extraordinary achievement in any field. Over past 20 years he has gathered compelling data about what great leaders and managers do differently and how high achievers find, cultivate and convert their strengths into performance and fulfilment.
