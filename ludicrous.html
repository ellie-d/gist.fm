How not to build a culture: Silicon Valley and the cult of the young white male

The Gist

Can culture fit lead to groupthink, discrimination and mediocrity? Former Fortune journalist and writer of TV show Silicon Valley Dan Lyons goes to work at well-known tech startup HubSpot and discovers that building a culture optimized for innovation can produce unintended and very disturbing consequences.

Based on: Disrupted: My Misadventure in the Start-Up Bubble

The Author

As a former tech editor at Newsweek and reporter at Forbes, Dan has spent two decades covering disruptive technologies from artificial intelligence to robotics and fusion energy. He's known online as The Fake Steve Jobs - a pseudonym he used to write a parody blog about Apple. In 2013 he joined Boston startup HubSpot for what he calls a "year in startup hell", which he later recounted in his bestselling book Disrupted.
